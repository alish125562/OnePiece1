# BEST CREATION OF ALL TIME
Written by Eiichiro Oda

ONE PIECE

Wealth, Fame, Power...Gold Roger, King of the pirates,
attained everything this world has to offer. The words
he uttered just before his death drove people to the
seas. And so men head for the Grand Line in pursuit of
their dreams! Then the world entered the Great Pirates Era!
